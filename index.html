About Us

Restaurant "Mimino", opened in 2000, will pleasantly surprise you with exquisite dishes of Georgian and European cuisine. Luxurious halls, beautifully served tables, a wide selection of dishes, attentive service and a cozy atmosphere will do

In 2009, a small hotel was opened for guests of our city at the "Mimino" restaurant, where you can rest comfortably in European-level rooms, enjoying silence and professional service. Hotel "Mimino" provides eight comfortable rooms with satellite TV, WI-FI Internet, air conditioners, telephones with direct access to city communication.
